FAQs

Still curious about what we do? Here are some of the questions we get asked most often. If your question isn't answered here, feel free to contact us directly and we'll get back to you asap.

What services does UZO LAB offer?

UZO LAB is a digital agency specializing in creating cutting-edge websites and digital experiences. We offer a range of services including website design, development, and management, brand identity development, and custom automation and software integrations, tailored to meet the unique needs of your business.

Check our full list of services for info.

How much will it cost to develop my website?

We offer flexible subscription models to fit various budgets. Our plans include 'Liftoff' at $2000/month and 'Growth' at $3000/month. For projects that don't fit into these plans, we also provide custom quotes.

What does a subscription to UZO LAB include?

All subscriptions include access to our on-demand design and development team, a project dashboard, weekly project updates, and a specified number of tasks per week, depending on your chosen plan.

Tasks can fit into a number of categories depending on the needs of your project. Please see our full list of services for more info.

How does the custom quote process work?

For projects that don’t fit our standard plans, you can request a custom quote. This involves a one-time payment (including a deposit at the start of the project timeline) for the entire project from start to finish, with no recurring subscription.

This is suitable for either very simple projects (Like landing pages) which can be delivered in 1/2 weeks - or for very complex projects (like e-commerce sites) that need custom solutions and more attention than the 'Growth' plan offers.
‍
You'll also receive a discount on future subscriptions.

What if I need more than one project in production at a time?

Our 'Growth' plan allows for two projects in production simultaneously. If you need more, you can discuss additional custom arrangements with our team. Your projects may be better suited to a custom quote or a custom subscription arrangement.

Please book a call so we can understand how best to help.

How do I get started with UZO LAB?

Getting started is simple! Book a free workshop call with our design team to discuss your audience, goals, and how we can make a significant impact on your business. From there, we will suggest the best way forward, whether it be choosing one of our existing design plans or crafting a custom quote tailored to your needs.

Can I change or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can pause, cancel, or change your subscription at any time. Our service is designed to be as flexible as your business needs.

What is the difference between the 'Liftoff' and 'Growth' plans?

The 'Liftoff' plan is suited for smaller-scale projects with a response time of 48 hours, handling up to 5 tasks per week.

The 'Growth' plan is designed for more intensive projects that require a faster time-to-market: offering priority response and the capacity to double the number of weekly tasks.

How can I track and manage my project?

You can easily manage and update your project through our intuitive project dashboard. This platform allows you to track progress, request changes, and communicate with our team asynchronously.

You will receive weekly updates via the dashboard, and the option to schedule a call once per week to discuss progress and plans going forward.

What results can I expect from working with UZO LAB?

Broadly speaking, by partnering with UZO LAB, you can expect an enhanced web presence, increased lead generation, and the ongoing support of a dedicated design & development team to help your business thrive in the digital space.

More specifically: you can expect us to deliver on anything you ask for, as long as it fits into the realm of design & technology. Our team has years of experience in the digital space and we pride ourselves on the quality of our work.
